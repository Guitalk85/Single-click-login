What changed
Today: 5 clicks · ~15-20 seconds · ends at a dead-end empty screen for everyone
Proposed: 1 auth click · lands directly at last-used bank's dashboard
Mechanism: server stores last_active_location_id per user, updated every time they switch banks. Restored on every login. Location pill in top nav available for switching — but is never a gate.
Same UI, different behavior across personas

One screen, one mechanism. The 80/20 split is about how often they switch, not what they see.

80% — Field user (WH Manager, Rural Ops)

Always works at one bank. Logs in → lands at their bank → never touches the location pill. Step 4 essentially never happens for them.

20% — Developers / internal team

Most of the time also working in one bank context. Occasionally uses Step 4 to switch banks for testing or debugging. The dropdown shows "Recent" banks at top so common switches are one tap.
